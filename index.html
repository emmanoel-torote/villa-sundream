Pedregal de Cabo San Lucas

The original
Cabo address.

Founded in the early 1970s, Pedregal was Cabo San Lucas' first planned luxury community. Built into granite hillsides above the marina, it remains the city's most recognized and sought-after residential address — home to the Resort at Pedregal, a private beach club, and a self-contained community unlike anywhere else in Los Cabos.
